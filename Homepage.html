Welcome to LINGAP

A groundbreaking initiative dedicated to preserving the rich linguistic tapestry of the Philippines. In a nation where Tagalog's dominance threatens the existence of numerous minority dialects, our project seeks to reverse this trend by providing a platform for their documentation and conservation. With a comprehensive approach, we offer a dynamic online space where users can actively engage in sharing knowledge and experiences related to their dialects through interactive forums. Through community collaboration, we are building an extensive e-library equipped with a user-friendly tagging system, facilitating easy navigation by dialect and region. Moreover, our innovative map feature visualizes the distribution of dialects, fostering awareness and appreciation for linguistic diversity. Committed to our mission, we operate as a non-profit organization, relying on donations from individuals, organizations, and institutions who share our passion for language preservation. We also aim to forge partnerships with academic institutions, government agencies, and cultural organizations to amplify our impact. We invite linguists, educators, technologists, and enthusiasts alike to join us in this vital endeavor. Whether through expertise, outreach, or financial support, your involvement can contribute significantly to safeguarding the invaluable heritage of Philippine languages for generations to come. Together, let's ensure that the voices of our diverse linguistic landscape continue to resonate and thrive.
